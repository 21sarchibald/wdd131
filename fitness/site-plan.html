Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website will be to give people a place to track their fitness goals. They will be able to input their workout details for the day along with their food and water consumption for the day. The website will store their stats for each workout and calculate their total calorie and protein intake for the day.

Audience

The website is made for people consistently looking for a place to track their daily activities and health achievements. It can be used of people of almost any age; it is intended for people who are consistently working out and wanting to track progress.

Dynamic elements

The workout page will provide a form with input fields to input the type of exercise, the name of the exercise, repetitions, and when the user did the exercise. The user can then submit the form, which will add it to a record of their workouts.

The food tracking page will provide a form with input fields to input the food, calories, and protein of each meal they eat. Just like the workout page, the form can be submitted and added to a list of the foods eaten for that day.

The pages will also contain light and dark modes that can be toggled on and off.

Content

Home page

Exercise is an important part of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Varying workout types and moving your body on a daily basis will help keep you mobile and healthy.

Cardio is a great way to keep your heart healthy and maintain overall fitness. It increases bloodflow and improves stamina. There are various forms of cardio (running, kickboxing, cycling, etc.) that can be enjoyed by many different types of people.

Lifting weights is a great way to maintain a healthy body. It strengthens joints, increases mobility, and increases overall strength. Lifting weights regularly is also a good way to prevent injury.

Meal Tracker

Another part of maintaining a healthy lifestyles is fueling your body with nutritious and quality food. Eating the right amount and getting enough nutrients will allow you to exercise efficiently and keep your body healthy longer.

Eating food with enough variety and color is crucial to maintaining a healthy diet. Eating different fruits and vegetables can help you to consume the essential vitamins and nutrients you need to stay healthy.

Eating enough protein is also crucial to staying healthy. Protein is especially crucial for building muscle as you exercise. It will allow you to have the energy and nutrients you need to increase overall fitness.
